About Maui Seaside Hotel

Welcome to Maui Seaside Hotel — your tropical escape located on the beautiful island of Maui, Hawaii. Whether you're here for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a business retreat, our hotel offers the perfect balance of luxury, relaxation, and convenience.

Room Types

  • Standard Room: Comfortable and affordable with garden view.
  • Deluxe Room: Spacious rooms with oceanfront views.
  • Executive Suite: Includes a private balcony, living space, and premium amenities.

Facilities & Services

  • Outdoor swimming pool
  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi
  • 24-hour front desk and concierge
  • On-site restaurant and beach bar
  • Fitness center and spa
  • Airport shuttle service

Nearby Attractions

  • Lahaina Historic Town (15 min drive)
  • Ka'anapali Beach (10 min walk)
  • Maui Ocean Center
  • Haleakalā National Park

We look forward to welcoming you to paradise. Enjoy the aloha spirit at Maui Seaside Hotel!
